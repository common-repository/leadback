== Description ==

Every website visitor is a potential customer. LeadBack allows you to call your customer for free in 27 seconds. The LeadBack widget lets you call your website visitors and helps you gain an additional 30-125% leads on the phone.

This plugin makes a simple widget for callback and live chat on your website. LeadBack its a services for the site, allowing you to increase the conversion to leads. Official LeadBack plugin.

= How it works =

1. Visitors visit your website, click on a callback button, enter their phone number.
2. The service quickly calls your company, and then immediately to the visitor himself.
3. Within 27 seconds, your employee communicates with the client and makes a sale!
4. The client is shocked by your promptness and is ready to spend the money.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the entire leadback folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings->LeadBack screen to configure the plugin.
